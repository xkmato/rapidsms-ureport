About U-report

U-report is a free SMS-based system that allows young South Sudanese to raise their voices and advocate for positive change in their new Nation

    U-report is made up of:
  • Weekly SMS messages and polls to and from a growing community of U-reporters.
  • Radio programmes featuring U-report poll results and stories from U-reporters throughout South Sudan
  • Articles in newspapers featuring stories generated by U-reporters
  • Features on social media platforms like facebook and Twitter to share stories from U-reporters in South Sudan with the rest of the world.
The mission of U-report

U-report was designed to help YOU inspire action for positive change in South Sudan..
Through U-report, young people across South Sudan can jointly articulate a vision for the future of their new Nation and work with the leaders of today to make this vision a reality.
Sign up. Take part. Be heard. With U-report, YOUR VOICE COUNTS.
